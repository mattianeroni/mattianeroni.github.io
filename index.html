About

I'm a reliable, friendly, energetic and resourceful research scientist with a Ph.D. in algorithms and artificial intelligence applied to logistics and manufacturing. I have over 5 years of experience in algorithms development, technical consulting, project management, logistics, supply chain, operations research, machine learning, web applications development. I toke part to more than 15 collaborations with different companies and approximately 25 scientific publications.

I have a high willingness to learn and desire to constantly prove myself carrying out more and more challenging tasks. I'm interested in working with people who are honest, direct, and without fake goodness.

I curretly work as Senior Optimization Engineer in Spindox and I previously worked mainly in academics as Researcher in several universities across Europe (i.e., University of Parma, Stuttgart Technology University of Applied Sciences, Universitat Oberta de Catalunya, University of Modena), and Adjunct Professor at University of Parma.

I like living in big cities (even better if close to the sea) and during my life I lived in Reggio Emilia (vote 6/10), London (vote 8/10), Barcelona (vote 9/10), Stuttgart (vote 4/10). I'm always looking for new oppotunities to relocate, make new experiences, and find my place in the world.

Projects

3-DimensionalCasePicking

An algorithm to optimize pickers routing and pallets loading

Accomplishments

A new algorithm for the 3-Dimensional Case Picking Problem. A newly considered problem of operational research that combines the well-known case picking problem with the positioning of 3-dimensional items inside pallets (i.e., Pallet Loading Problem). The idea comes from a collaboration with the IN3 research group in Computer Science Dept. at Universitat Oberta de Catalunya (Barcelona, SPAIN).

Server Side Algorithms

A platform where users can upload their algorithms and developers can find the algorithms they want

Accomplishments

Server Side Algorithms (SSA) is a platform with the objective to cover the gap between applications developers and scientists. Through a standard API, the registered developers are allowed to send HTTP requests to the server by requiring the execution of a specific algorithm previously uploaded by a different user.

Derma

A mobile application for skin deseases detection

Accomplishments

Derma is a mobile application for Android and iOS for detection and recognition of skin deseases. It is able to detect from a picture 9 different kinds of skin desease, and the classification is carried out through a fine tuned EfficientNet model. The application is therefore making use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) to identify an eventual skin illness.

SimJS

A micro discrete event simulation library in NodeJS

Accomplishments

A library for discrete events simulation in JavaScript (Node.js) and inspired by Simpy (Python). The library provides:

  • Simulation environment
  • Customizable processes
  • Resources management
Milk Logistics

Implementation and validation of several algorithms to optimise the collection of milk and its delivery to the production plant or cheese factory.

Accomplishments

Project born from the collaboration with University of Campania "Luigi Vanvitelli" and several Parmigiano Reggiano cheese producer in the area of Parma (Italy). It provides 6 algorithms to optimize the shipments of milk from farmers to the production plant.

Car Sharing

Implementation of an algorithm for solving the Multi-Source Team Orienteering Problem applied to car sharing

Accomplishments

On occasion of this research, we faced an extension of the original Team Orienteering Problem (TOP), in which the vehicles start from many different sources and visit a set of customers before reaching the destination. This problem finds several application especially in car sharing and ride sharing optimization. A real implementation to improve the ride sharing in Barcelona city has also been done.

Automated Warehouse Optimization

Simulation and optimization of a Shuttle-Lift-Crane based Automated Storage and Retrieval System

Accomplishments

On occasion of the collaboration with Matter, I developed the simulation of a complex automated warehouse widely used in the steel sector (i.e., Shuttle-Lift-Crane based AS/RS). I also developed a web application to customize the parameters of the simulation and save the results. A set of algorithms to improve the performance have then been developed (for privacy reasons is not possible to make them open-source).
